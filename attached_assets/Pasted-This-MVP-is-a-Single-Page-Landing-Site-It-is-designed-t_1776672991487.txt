This MVP is a Single-Page Landing Site. It is designed to act as a highly polished, high-converting digital storefront. Instead of a complex site with multiple pages to navigate, it tells a linear story as the user scrolls, guiding them directly from their first impression to getting in touch.

Here is exactly what this website is and how it functions for Artheswara Raya:

1. The "Hook" (Hero Section)
What it is: The very first thing a visitor sees—a massive, premium background image of a stunning interior, a bold headline, and a primary button.

The Purpose: Immediate impact. It establishes the company as a premium, high-quality brand within two seconds and gives visitors an immediate action to take ("Get a Free Consultation").

2. The "Trust Builder" (About Us & Clients)
What it is: A brief narrative about their craftsmanship alongside a banner of client logos (which can act as placeholders for now).

The Purpose: It answers why someone should hire them. It establishes credibility and shows that they are an experienced, trusted entity.

3. The "Offering" (Services)
What it is: A clean, three-part grid breaking down what they actually do: Kitchen Sets, Bedroom Sets, and Full Interior Solutions.

The Purpose: Clarity. It prevents visitors from having to guess if Artheswara handles the specific type of room they want renovated.

4. The "Proof" (Projects Gallery)
What it is: A visually heavy, grid-style portfolio showing off their best photographic work.

The Purpose: In interior design, seeing is believing. This is the core of the MVP. It allows their work to speak for itself without requiring the user to click away to a separate, slow-loading portfolio page.

5. The "Conversion" (Contact Section)
What it is: The final destination at the bottom of the page, featuring a contact form and a highly visible WhatsApp chat button.

The Purpose: Lead generation. By placing the WhatsApp button prominently, it caters to how most local business inquiries are handled—fast, direct messaging straight to the business owner's phone.

The Strategic Value
Because this MVP is built entirely with raw HTML and Tailwind CSS via CDN, it has zero moving parts. There is no database to maintain or server logic to configure right now. It is incredibly lightweight, meaning it will load almost instantly.

When you are ready to push it live, this single-file structure is perfectly optimized to drop straight into Cloudflare Pages for instant, seamless deployment and lightning-fast edge hosting. It gives the client a professional web presence immediately, proving your momentum on the project, while leaving plenty of room to build out more complex features later.
